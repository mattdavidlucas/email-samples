Looking for a seamless integration between your PubNub realtime data and Microsoft Azure?

Now with the PubNub Realtime Gateway for Microsoft Azure you can:
• Transport realtime bi-directional data streams to Azure from over 70 supported platforms via PubNub.
• Choose to terminate your realtime data streams inside of Azure, or provide a realtime, bi-directional bridge back out to your PubNub devices.
• Add visualizations on top of realtime data streams using Power BI.
Sneak peeks are being scheduled by request, and we’re looking for showcase partners for an official launch announcement. Let us know if you are interested in learning more!
